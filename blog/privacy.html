Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

Crispy Status ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our video optimization service at crispystatus.com and our Android application.

2. Video Processing

All video processing occurs entirely on your device using WebAssembly technology. Your videos are never uploaded to our servers. We do not have access to, store, or transmit any video content you process through our service.

3. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage data: Anonymous statistics about feature usage (number of videos processed, processing times) stored locally on your device via localStorage.
  • Device information: Basic browser and device information for compatibility purposes (browser type, screen size, operating system).
  • Advertising: We display ads through Google AdSense, which may collect information as described in Google's privacy policy.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your videos or any media files
  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Browsing history

5. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB to store:

  • App preferences (watermark settings, install prompt status)
  • Cached FFmpeg engine files (for faster subsequent loads)
  • Anonymous usage counters

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies and device identifiers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr/unpkg CDN: For delivering the FFmpeg WebAssembly engine. These CDNs may log access data per their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@crispystatus.com
