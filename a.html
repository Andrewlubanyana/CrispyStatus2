About Crispy Status

Crispy Status is a free, browser-based tool that optimizes your videos for WhatsApp Status. We use a WhatsApp-native encoding engine that matches WhatsApp's internal compression target, so your videos stay sharp and clear after posting.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to share sharp, clear videos on WhatsApp Status — regardless of their phone, internet connection, or technical knowledge. Crispy Status makes professional-grade video optimization accessible to everyone, for free.

🔒 Privacy First

Your videos never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology. We don't upload, store, or have access to any of your videos. What you process stays on your phone.

Zero data collection on your videos. We can't see your videos. We don't want to see your videos. They're yours.

⚙️ Technology

Crispy Status uses FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly (WASM) to process videos entirely in your browser. This means:

  • No server-side processing — everything runs on your device
  • No file uploads — your video never leaves your phone
  • Works offline after the first load
  • No app installation required (also available on Google Play)

📊 How It Works

Instead of fighting WhatsApp's video compression, Crispy Status matches it. We encode your video at the same resolution (640p), bitrate (700-2200 kbps), and format (H.264 Main profile) that WhatsApp's internal encoder targets. The result? WhatsApp sees your video as "already optimized" and barely re-compresses it.
